Tips to Travel on a Budget

May 15-16, 2018

Hall of Mirrors, Versailles, France

The yearn to travel on a slim stack of cash seems impossible. Trust me, I am a broke college student; I can relate. However, there are ways to make your dreams turn into a reality with these simple steps:

Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore, Florence, Italy

First off, don't be intimidated by the costly prices that pop up on your screen. Take some time figuring out a budget. From there, set a prince range when you go to find a destination. That way you won't find yourself spending more than you can afford.
